About DIADICTOR

The project uses ML application with a backend in flask. The DiaDictor is basically Diabetes +Predictor and is used to get the probability of diabetes type-2. The results shown are pure predictions which are calculated by analyzing a data set. Our project is for those who wish to know the chances of being affected by this condition before referring to a doctor or those who want to double verify their reports.
