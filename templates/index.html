About Diabetes


Diabetes mellitus, commonly known as diabetes, is a metabolic disease that causes high blood sugar. The hormone insulin moves sugar from the blood into your cells to be stored or used for energy. With diabetes, your body either doesn’t make enough insulin or can’t effectively use the insulin it does make.

Untreated high blood sugar from diabetes can damage your nerves, eyes, kidneys, and other organs.

About Project

Diabetes is one of the most fatal chronic condition. Our project is made solely for people of India.

The project uses ML application with a backend in flask. The DiaDictor is basically Diabetes +Predictor and is used to get the probability of diabetes type-2. The results shown are pure predictions which are calculated by analyzing a data set. Our project is for those who wish to know the chances of being affected by this condition before referring to a doctor or those who want to double verify their reports.


How to Use?



First Step

Get the reports
  • Glucose
  • Blood pressure
  • Skin thickness
  • Insulin
  • BMI
  • diabetes pedigree function

Second Step

Enter the details
  • Click the check button
  • Enter the mentioned details to get a probable result.
  • Click on predict button


Third Step

Take actions
  • If the predictor shows a probability of more than 70 % then immediately consult a doctor.
  • If the predictor shows a probability less than 70% then maintain your health.
